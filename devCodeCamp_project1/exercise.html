Fighting Stress with Stress

Believe it or not, stress can be a positive thing. There are two types of stress, Distress and Eustress.

Distress is what usually comes to mind when the word stress is mentioned. It is negetive stress which is characterized by:

  • Causing Fear and Anxiety
  • Feels Unpleasant
  • May develope mental and/or physical problems
  • Decrease in productivity and mood

Examples of things that cause Distress:

  • Work related issues
  • Car accident
  • A break-up
  • Spilled milk

The positive stress that you can benefit from is called Eustress. Characteristics of Eustress are:

  • Feeling Motivated
  • Causes excitement
  • Increases mood and performance

Examples of Eustress activities:

  • Working out
  • Yoga
  • Riding a rollercoaster
  • Karaoke
