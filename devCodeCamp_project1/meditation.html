What is Meditation?

A simple definition of meditation is to engage in contemplation or reflection. Essentially, one meditates to achieve a state of thoughtless awareness. It is having a calm mind, while being alert or conscious.

Benefits of Meditation include:

  • Reduce Stress(distress)
  • Increased cardiovascular and immune health
  • Improved consentration
  • Improves mood
