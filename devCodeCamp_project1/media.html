Relax with Entertainment

Taking the time to walk away from a stressful situation and relaxing by watching TV or a movie is an easy way to relieve stress. It may not be beneficial to watch a horror film or the news because it can cause more stress and anxiety.

Another way to relaxing using media is by playing video games, preferably something easy that doesn't take much thought. A few games I like to play to destress is REZ HD, Toss the Turtle, Candy Crush and AbyssRium. What sets these games apart from GTA, Halo, or Call of Duty is they aren't as stress inducing, but if those games work for you by all means play them. Listening to music is also a great way to calm the nerves. I will typically listen to classical, hip hop, or drum and bass when I want to kick back after a stressful day, or just to put me in a relaxed state before falling asleep.
